This passion led to the creation of my DirectX 11–based 3D engine, which I make available for download here. It is designed as a technical development 
project with the goal of making rendering and engine development more accessible through a clear and approachable API.
Engine now supports multiple lights with efficient GPU-based light culling. Forward+ architecture delivers high performance, full MSAA support, and seamless transparency. Scalable from one to hundreds of dynamic lights.
